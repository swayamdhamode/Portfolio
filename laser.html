My learning in 3D Design
from day 1 to day 8

Day 1.

Introduction to Laser Cutting and Safety Protocols

- Introduction to the module objectives and structure.
- Overview of laser cutting technology and its applications.
- Explanation of safety protocols specific to laser cutting.
- Discussion on the importance of safety gear and machine precautions.

day 2.

Laser Cutting Machines and Equipment

- Explanation of the components and operation of laser cutting machines.
- Introduction to different types of laser cutting machines (CO2, fiber, etc.).
- Demonstration of machine setup, calibration, and maintenance procedures.
- Overview of auxiliary equipment required for laser cutting.

day 3.

Materials for Laser Cutting

- Introduction to materials suitable for laser cutting (cardboard, wood, acrylic, metal, etc.).
- Discussion on the properties of various materials and their interaction with lasers.
- Considerations for material thickness, density, and cutting parameters.
- Hands-on demonstration of cutting different materials using laser technology.

day 4.

Fusion 360 for Laser Cutting

- Introduction to Parametric Design using Fusion 360 software.
- Basics of creating designs for laser cutting using vector graphics.
- Explanation of design considerations such as scale, orientation, and layout.
- Hands-on practice with designing simple shapes and patterns for laser cutting.

day 5.

Advanced CAD Techniques

- Exploring more complex design techniques for laser cutting.
- Introduction to nesting parts to optimize material usage.
- Incorporating text, images, and geometric features into laser cutting designs.
- Troubleshooting common design issues and refining designs for production.

day 6.

Preparing Designs for Laser Cutting

- Overview of laser cutting software for preparing designs.
- Explanation of importing designs, setting cutting parameters, and toolpath generation.
- Hands-on practice with laser cutting software to prepare designs for production.
- Previewing and simulating laser cuts to ensure accuracy and efficiency.

day 7.

Hands-On Laser Cutting Session

- Review of safety protocols and reminders.
- Hands-on practice with operating laser cutting machines.
- Cutting designs created in previous sessions and troubleshooting production issues.
- Guidance on post-processing techniques such as cleaning and finishing cuts.

day 8.

Project Showcase and Reflection

- Presentation of projects created during the module.
- Reflection on the learning process, challenges faced, and lessons learned.
- Discussion on potential applications of laser cutting in various industries.
- Recap of key takeaways and resources for further exploration of laser cutting technology.
